Savory

Andhra Pradesh is a state in southern India, and its cuisine is famous for its spicy and flavorful dishes. One of the most iconic dishes is the Hyderabadi Biryani, a fragrant rice dish cooked with meat (usually chicken or mutton), aromatic spices, and saffron. Another popular dish is the Gongura Pachadi, made with tangy sorrel leaves and spices.

Lunch

Andhra Pradesh, lunch is a hearty and satisfying affair. A traditional meal called "Andhra Bhojanam" typically consists of rice, sambar (a lentil-based vegetable stew), rasam (a tangy soup), pappu (dal), a variety of vegetable curries, and curd (yogurt). Breakfast

For breakfast in Andhra Pradesh, you can enjoy a variety of delicious dishes! One popular option is the pesarattu, a crepe made from green gram (moong dal) batter, usually served with ginger chutney. Another classic breakfast dish is the famous Andhra dosa, a thin and crispy pancake made from fermented rice and lentil batter, served with coconut chutney and sambar.

Dinner

Andhra Pradesh, dinner is often a lighter meal compared to lunch. People enjoy a variety of dishes like pulihora (tamarind rice), curd rice, and chapatis with vegetable curries. Seafood lovers can indulge in delicious fish and prawn preparations like chepala pulusu (fish curry) and royyala vepudu (prawn fry). And if you're in the mood for something spicy, you can try the famous Andhra-style chicken or mutton biryani.
